About

Vinayak Siddha College (VSC) is an institution having academic collaboration with Progressive English Secondary School (PESS) is highly dedicated to higher education suitable for the 21st century. It is founded by a team of experts having prolong engagement in educational sector more than two decades with the motto "Committed for Quality Education". VSC is well set up in a sound atmosphere and dedicated to disseminate quality education pertaining to the needs and expectation of digitally savvy era of 21st century.
